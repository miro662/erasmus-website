Cultural-lingual preparation

Before departing for internship students made Europass CV in English. It was sent to mediating and accepting agencies in order to select places of internship.

Students have prepared for internship for several months before departure. Most important part of preparation was Cultural-lingual preparation led by Maria Materna - 30 hours of meetings, where students improved their techincal English.

A lot of organizational meetings about project took place. Conditions of project, its tasks and targets and preparation for travel was discussed during those meetings. Meetings with safety instructor and psychologist were also organized.

This preparation helped participants during internship - not only during work, but also in their everyday life in Italy.

Cultural-lingual programme organized during stay in Brscia was continuation of preparation in Poland. It was organized with active support of affiliated mediating agency Mistral, with big engagement of its CEO, Mr. Luigi , international projects coordinator - Ms. Antonella and Mr. Stefano .
